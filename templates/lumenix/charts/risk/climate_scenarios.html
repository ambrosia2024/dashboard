Data assumptions – climate-adjusted scenarios

Data assumptions

  • Data is simulated for the crop–pathogen pair currently selected (labels from local storage if present, otherwise Lettuce - Salmonella).
  • The period is fixed to monthly 2025-01 → 2035-12 to show a medium-term climate signal.
  • Each scenario applies a different warming slope (Optimistic/Central → RCP4.5, Pessimistic → RCP8.5).
  • Monthly temperature anomaly (Δ °C) is the main “climate driver”; probability of illness (%) is nudged in the same direction to illustrate sensitivity, not to predict cases.
  • No real climate model output is read here; values are for demo/exploration only.
